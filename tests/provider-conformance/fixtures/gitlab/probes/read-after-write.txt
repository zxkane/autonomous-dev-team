## What the probe would capture live

  PUT /projects/:id/issues/:iid  {"add_labels":"pending-review","remove_labels":"in-progress"}
  → 200 OK, body includes `.labels: ["autonomous","pending-review"]` immediately.

  GET /projects/:id/issues?labels=pending-review  (issued within 1s)
  → the just-transitioned issue appears in the returned array.

Confirms GitLab has NO Asana-style eventual-consistency lag between
label transition and label-filtered enumeration. The dispatcher's next-tick
list_pending_review call sees the transition immediately.
